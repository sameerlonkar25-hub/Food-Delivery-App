# Hunny Food – Food Delivery App UI Prototype 🍔📱

Hunny Food is a high-fidelity mobile food delivery application prototype designed in **Figma** as part of our **Project Based Learning (PBL)** final project during the **first year of engineering**.
The project was created collaboratively by a team of **4 students** with the aim of designing a modern, clean, and user-friendly food ordering experience focused on simplicity, speed, and visual appeal.

The application is targeted primarily towards **college students and young working professionals** who frequently order food online and prefer a fast, transparent, and smooth ordering process.

# ✨ Design Philosophy

The UI design of CraveIt is based on four core principles:

* **Simple Navigation** – Minimal and intuitive screen flow
* **Fast Ordering Experience** – Reduced friction in browsing and checkout
* **Transparent Pricing** – Clear billing and delivery details
* **Warm Visual Identity** – Friendly and food-inspired aesthetics

The app uses a distinctive **terracotta and cream color palette** to create a warm and approachable visual identity.
A custom-designed logo and consistent typography system were used throughout the prototype to maintain branding consistency.

# 🛠️ Tools Used

* **Figma** – UI/UX Design and Prototyping
* Design Components & Auto Layout
* Interactive Prototyping Features
* Custom Color System and Typography
